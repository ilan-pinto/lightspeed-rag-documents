Compliance Operator overview
"Red Hat OpenShift Container Platform" Compliance Operator (CO) runs compliance scans and provides remediations to assist users in meeting compliance standards. For the latest updates, see the Compliance Operator release notes. If needed, you can engage Red Hat support.

The Compliance Operator does not automatically perform remediations. Ensuring compliance standards are met is required by the user.
